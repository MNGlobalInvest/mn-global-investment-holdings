# MN Global Investment Holdings, LLC
## Protocol-Driven Bitcoin Acquisition & Custody

### Overview
**MN Global Investment Holdings, LLC** operates as a protocol-driven organization with a singular focus: the systematic acquisition and secure custody of Bitcoin. This repository represents the complete operational and governance framework of the company where documentation, protocols, and organizational structure exist as code and policy.

**The repository represents the business. The business lives in the repository.**

---

### ₿ Mission & Vision

**Mission**  
To acquire and securely hold Bitcoin through automated, protocol-driven systems designed to maximize long-term accumulation while minimizing discretionary decision-making.

**Vision**  
To operate as a self-scaling, strategically aligned organization where every system, process, and decision reinforces continuous Bitcoin accumulation through autonomous, resilient, and technologically advanced operations.

---

### ₿ Core Operating Principles

**Single-Minded Purpose**  
All actions and capital allocation decisions are measured exclusively by their impact on long-term Bitcoin holdings.

**100% Bitcoin Reserve Policy**  
All cash flows and profits are systematically converted into Bitcoin, maintaining a pure Bitcoin-denominated balance sheet.

**Protocol-Driven Operations**  
Automated systems and predefined procedures minimize subjective decision-making and enhance operational reliability.

**Self-Scaling Infrastructure**  
Revenue streams, operational systems, and technology platforms are engineered to scale without proportional increases in overhead.

**Security & Resilience**  
Advanced custody methods including multi-signature cold storage, compartmentalized permissions, and continuous monitoring protect all assets.

**Transparency & Auditability**  
All critical systems, processes, and decisions are documented for oversight and accountability, supporting both internal governance and external review.
